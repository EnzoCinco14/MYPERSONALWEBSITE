My Favorite Movies

John Wick

Genre: Action, Crime, Thriller

Release Year: 2014

I love John Wick for its stylish action choreography, intense gun-fu sequences, and Keanu Reeves' compelling performance. The film's unique world-building of the assassin's underground and its relentless pace makes it a standout in the action genre.

The Purge

Genre: Horror, Thriller, Sci-Fi

Release Year: 2013

I love The Purge for its thought-provoking premise and intense social commentary. The film's exploration of human nature during a night where all crime is legal creates a terrifying yet fascinating look at society, morality, and survival.
